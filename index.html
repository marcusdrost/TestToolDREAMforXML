Solutions for data analysis and regression testing

We are developing methods and tooling for data analysis and regression testing of databases and data warehouses or just csv or xml. Because comparing data sets and finding root causes of differences is not easy. Check sums and random samples are often good enough but for data sensitive systems more sophisticated solutions are necessary to analyze data related problems more accurate and faster.
